About the Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It is made up of billions of stars, planets, gas, and dust. From Earth, it appears as a glowing band across the night sky. a large barred spiral galaxy, roughly 100,000 to 200,000 light-years across, containing 100-400 billion stars, including our Sun
