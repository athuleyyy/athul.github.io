SOLAR SYSTEM GUIDE

The eight
planets

Our Solar System sits inside the Milky Way. It has rocky inner planets, giant outer planets, moons, rings, asteroids, and one star at the centre: the Sun.

PLANET CARDS

Quick facts

Mercury

The closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our Solar System.

Venus

A hot rocky planet with thick clouds and a surface hidden from view.

Earth

The only known planet with life, covered by oceans, land, and atmosphere.

Mars

The red planet, known for dust storms, volcanoes, valleys, and polar ice caps.

Learn more about Mars

Jupiter

The largest planet, with huge storms and many moons.

Saturn

A gas giant famous for its wide, bright rings made of ice and rock.

Uranus

An ice giant that spins on its side compared with most other planets.

Neptune

A distant ice giant with powerful winds and a deep blue colour.
